Atom Bank Student Perks App

Description

  • Dustin Guillemin

  • Newcastle Upon Tyne, UK

  • Spring 2017

In an externship with Atom Bank during a semester abroad, our team was approached by UX and Marketing with the challenge of envisioning a new product targeting university students. The idea for appealing to this demographic was a subscription-based rewards system where students could unlock discounts for stores and services. Each purchase with a promotional redemption would allow the student to work toward a cash-based reward from the bank. Working through the iterative design cycle, we brainstormed and prototyped a concept application. Visuals were produced in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and brought together with Proto.io to create our final Mock-up within the 40 hours of allotted time.
