Turbo Pinball Ultra Pinball Machine

Description

  • Dustin Guillemin

  • Ewing, New Jersey

  • 2017-2018

A Pinball Machine? In an era where digital gaming is increasing in popularity, does physical gaming stand a chance? For Senior Thesis, this was the question we sought out to answer with Turbo Pinball Ultra. The solution was to merge physical and digital, creating an interaction between traditional pinball gameplay with projection mapping for the visuals and display. The ball would interact with physical components and connections through Arduino, reading values into Unity. In the end, the static visual elements were designed in Adobe Illustrator and responsive elements in Adobe After Effects. Our presentation proved that there is a place for interactive nostalgia among modern gaming.
