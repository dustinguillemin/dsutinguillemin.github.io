White Label Print Shop

Description

  • Dustin Guillemin

  • 2022-2023

In 2022, just one year after starting my new role, I was appointed to lead the design for the Synchronoss White Label print shop redesign. This project would be deemed an anchor feature for the 2022-2023 design cycle, with major implications for Tier 1 clients in the U.S. and Japan. This was my largest project, consisting of north star designs with little input from product management that were refined to produce an achievable MVP. This project would be my main priority for most of 2022 with extensive hi-fidelity mockups, weekly meetings with developers and the PO, and several presentations to external and internal stakeholders. The project was well received by senior leadership at Synchronoss and with Verizon and Softbank.
